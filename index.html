    Target is a convenient, all-in-one store for groceries, household essentials, electronics, and even clothing. It’s a great spot to get everything you need for your dorm or apartment. With competitive pricing, Target is a favorite for students. It’s located at 1341 Boylston St, just a short walk or a quick bus ride from Northeastern University.
    View on Google Maps

    Best Buy is the go-to spot for all your electronics needs. From laptops and phones to home appliances and tech gadgets, they have it all. Whether you're setting up a home office or just need the latest tech, Best Buy at 401 Park Dr offers excellent deals for students, plus in-store tech support and repairs if you run into issues.
    View on Google Maps

    TJ Max offers fashionable clothing, shoes, accessories, and home goods at discounted prices. Whether you're looking for a wardrobe update or need decor to personalize your space, TJ Max is a budget-friendly choice. It’s conveniently located at 350 Washington St, perfect for students who want style on a budget.
    View on Google Maps

    Stop And Shop is a popular grocery store chain offering a wide selection of fresh produce, meats, dairy, and household essentials. Whether you’re meal-prepping or just grabbing snacks, Stop And Shop has what you need at affordable prices. Find this store at 1620 Tremont St, easily accessible from Northeastern's campus.
    View on Google Maps

    Walmart is a one-stop shop offering everything from groceries and electronics to furniture and cleaning supplies. Known for its unbeatable prices, Walmart is perfect for students who need to stock up on everyday essentials without breaking the bank. The store is located at 700 Pleasant St, and is well worth the trip for bulk purchases.
    View on Google Maps

    If you prefer organic and health-conscious grocery options, Whole Foods is the place for you. With a selection of organic produce, ethically sourced meat, and unique health products, Whole Foods caters to those with a focus on sustainable and wholesome living. It’s located at 181 Cambridge St, a convenient location for grabbing premium groceries close to campus.
    View on Google Maps

    Costco is ideal for bulk purchases, offering everything from groceries to home essentials at discounted prices. It's perfect for students looking to stock up on items for the long haul. The membership-based store is located at 2 Mystic View Rd, and while it’s a bit of a drive, the savings you’ll get on bulk items are worth it.
    View on Google Maps
